# Customer Behavior & Revenue Analysis (Python)

## Objective
Analyze customer behavior, revenue drivers and churn risk using Python.

## Tools
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib / Seaborn

## Key Analyses
- Revenue & KPI analysis
- Customer lifetime value
- Product category performance
- Churn risk detection
- Acquisition channel efficiency

## Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of customers
- Electronics generate the highest revenue
- Low engagement customers show strong churn signals
- Organic & Referral channels bring higher-value customers
